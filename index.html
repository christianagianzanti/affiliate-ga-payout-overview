Rate modeling & cost impact

in progress

How GA rates are set, what we honor through the transition, and what it costs

Theory behind the model

Start from what we pay each partner today, express it against their cohorted gross-adds (GA), and round down to the nearest $5 to set the GA rate. Rounding down keeps the program conservative while keeping partners roughly whole through the transition.

Full Price is honored through the tail. Any Free Trial logged before the Oct 1, 2026 transition stays on the old Full Price model — we keep commissioning those shops as they mature to full price for up to 200 days, so the last pre-transition cohort pays out around Apr 2027. Free Trials logged from Oct 1 onward pay on the GA model.

03

Clawback mechanism

Explained by Engineering

The clawback event fires 20 days after a paid trial is paid: if the shop has cancelled or frozen, the up-front commission is reversed back off the partner.

  • •Scope: starts with the 5 managed pay-on-trial partners
  • •Skips trials that have already been reversed (no double clawback)
